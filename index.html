To host a web page on Google Drive:
  1. Create a folder in Google Drive and set the sharing permission to " Public on the Web. "
  2. Upload the HTML, JavaScript and CSS files for your web page to the new folder.
  3. Open https://www.drv.tw/
  4. Select " Host on google drive "
  5. Choose your account
  6. Allow DriveToWeb to see, edit, create, and delete all of your Google Drive files
  7. Allow DriveToWeb to see and download all your Google Drive files
  8. Name your site in format [ < 2022 > < course > < year > < section > -lastname-firstname]
  9. e.g. 2022infotech2a-villareal-aljohn
  10. Click "Set Site Name"
  11. Reload the page.
  12. Open the URL provided. (it will look like https://...on.drv.tw/Site/)
  13. Share the URL and anyone can view your web
